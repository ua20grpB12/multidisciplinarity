PROCESS

MINDMAP

We have conducted several brainstorming sessions on the corona virus and its impact on society and individuals. We first tried this individually and after that in group. After we had discussed, reduced and merged the individual brainstorms into one mind map, we subdivided the points from the brainstorms into a number of topics and worked this out digitally. To make this mindmap clear and structured, we made a division of five subjects, into which we can roughly divide the points. For each point in the mindmap, we determined on what scale we believe this will have the greatest impact; macro level, meso level or micro level. 

CONCLUSION

In consultation we have chosen one subject on which we would focus on in function of the assignment. We noticed that the social aspect appeals to us the most. A possible reason for this could be that it is often a reflex for us humans to lean on our loved ones in difficult times. Because of COVID-19 we find ourselves in a strange situation where this is not entirely possible. From all sides we are being told that we must work together to fight COVID-19. If we do not work together and do not follow the imposed rules together, we won't get there. This results in a contradiction between togetherness and keeping a distance. The reality is that we are on our own together. Therefore, the aspect we want to focus on in this casestudy is alone vs. together.
