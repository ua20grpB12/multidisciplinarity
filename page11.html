MODEL

With all the preliminary research taken into consideration and keeping in mind, we continued the dialogue. This resulted in a series of thoughts, spins and scraps full of sketches, drawings,
scribbles, words and themes. All these thoughts made concise and merged have led to two models that form the framework for the to be further elaborated project.

MODEL 1

This model is a schematic representation of the structure we want to use for our concept. This is not an absolute scheme, it only becomes more concrete during the a further and more specific process. However, the foundation has been laid with this. The schedule consists of number of pillars that form the framework from which we will depart and form the mainstay for our process. 

MODEL 2

This diagram is created by combining the pillars of the mainstay of our process. We want to work with moving images and different dimensions that follow each other. The structure of these images can be made clear with this diagram. The numbers represent certain images and shots that we want to implement.
