CONCEPT

SPATIAL CONTEXT

In our case, the spatial context is a bit more complex. It is more than just the framework of a physical space and can rather be seen as an observation of society. We work with multiple dimensions and a predominantly philosophical approach. In this case, the spatial context can be expressed in a diagram. This scheme covers the entire concept and the final product that emanates from it. This end product serves the process towards it.The schematic representation of the domains below is a preliminary stage on which the final scheme is based. The latter, coupled with a substantiating and fundamental text, follows this.

“Is the digital era a vaccine against the impact of COVID-19 on our socializing society?" 

The hypothesis outlines a metaphorical vaccine to mask the initial problem that arose. In which physical daily confrontations, which occur in the contemporary, invalidate our well-known perception and association of the well-known.

Established and created social definitions are acutely contrary to the present. In which tertiary experiences are enforced a primary approach and vice versa.
A connection of man and space within a context built by time makes the difference between being alone, being together and being alone together fade away. The spatial literacy of the digital and physical dimensions provide the basis for this fusion.

Private spaces and (semi) public spaces can be entered both physically and digitally by an individual, a human cohesion and a cohesion of individuals. Possible in any coupling.

The response of the virus to our way of life is being answered and masked by one, by being in several dimensions in one time fragmentation. One figures his own presence.

Can our conversation as humans with space and time make the virus listen and obey?
